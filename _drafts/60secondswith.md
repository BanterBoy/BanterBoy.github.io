**3. What do you enjoy most about your job?**

> The problem-solving, without question. There's something deeply satisfying about automating a task that used to eat hours of my day — not just to make things quicker, but so that nobody ever has to do it again. I can't stand repeating the same task twice. Life's too short for conveyor-belt work.

**4. If you could change one thing about the rail industry, what would it be?**

> It'd be the assumption that everyone's online and on social media. When something goes wrong, updates tend to head to Twitter first — but I've stood on a platform with blank screens and no announcements, not knowing what's happening. I work in IT and I'm barely on social media myself, so I do think there's still a place for just telling people things at the point where they're standing.
