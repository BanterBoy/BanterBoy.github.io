## What Skills Should Know

- **copywriting**: Posts are not conversion pages. Apply voice/tone guidance, not CTA frameworks. The "product" is Luke's expertise; the "conversion" is the reader bookmarking the post or following on GitHub.
- **content-strategy**: Topics come from real work Luke is doing. The editorial calendar is loose — post when there's something worth sharing. Avoid manufactured content calendars.
- **seo-audit**: Titles and headings matter. Meta descriptions matter. Internal linking is weak (opportunity). Schema markup is not currently implemented.
- **ai-seo**: blog.lukeleigh.com publishes technical how-to and PowerShell module content — exactly the content types AI systems cite for "how to" queries. Worth optimising heading structure and definition blocks.
- **schema-markup**: Jekyll/Minimal Mistakes does not inject JSON-LD automatically. BlogPosting and BreadcrumbList schemas would be the highest-value additions.
